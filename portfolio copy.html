Portfolio Work

Project Description: When worlds collide unexpectedly, the demons invade and a battle on two fronts begins. Two unexpected heroes emerge, the unstoppable Doom Slayer and the American hero Duke Nukem must work together to save humanity and discover a way back to fix the rift between their worlds.


Key Features:

Coop Gameplay: Players can work together to complete puzzles, battle enemies and slay bosses.

Game Enemies: Players will be able to experience enemies from both worlds.

Unique Story: As players fight back the enemies, they will be able to slowly discover what really happened.

Iconic Music/SFX: Iconic game music and SFX that players will remember are included.

Unique Character Gameplay: Each player will experience gameplay differently based on their character DOOM GUY camera movement will be locked on the x-axis whereas Duke Nukem camera movement will have both axes unlocked.
