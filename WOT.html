Whispers Of Truth

This video displays the content and features inside the game.

Game Description: You are a detective tasked with investigating a home connected to the disappearance of a child. Things seem normal initially, but the further you explore, the more you realise something supernatural is happening. With the supernatural forces against you, solve the puzzles, discover the truth and escape.

Key Features:

Exploration & Puzzles: Players can explore the level for notes, items and even puzzles to complete.

Enemy: Players will have to try and avoid the roaming enemy or they can use an item to stun the enemy for a second.

Story: Players can explore and discover notes, they can learn more about the story of the game.

Encounters: Various sound and visual effects can occur at any moment to create tension.
