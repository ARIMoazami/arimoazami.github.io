CFS Educational Prototype

This video displays the content and features inside the short prototype game.

Project Description: A Day in the life experience of someone with CFS. Players can experience a small amount of what people with CFS must go through every day and how they can learn to manage the effects of it.

Project Research Aim: The aim of this project will be to educate more people about CFS and how it can affect the lives of people that live with this condition. When creating a serious game and having the aim to be teaching others about a topic it is important to view other sources or games. There are various types of serious games, Educational, Medical, Emotional Impact, Fitness and training.
