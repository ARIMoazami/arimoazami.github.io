DOOM UE5 Video

This video displays the current progress of the DOOM remake.

Current Implementations:
- Basic Main Menu
- Customizable Collectables (Keycards, Ammo, Powerups, Armour and Health)
- Weapons (Pistol, Shotgun, SuperShotgun and Chaingun)
- Door Interactions
- Enemy Classes
- Radiation Fields
- Sounds (Music and SFX)

The customizable collectables can be dragged and dropped into a scene and you can select which type you want from the original Doom games. Enemies and Doors are similar to the collectables so they could be adjusted to spawn certain types of enemies or whether a door requires a keycard to be opened.
