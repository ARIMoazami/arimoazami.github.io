About

From a young age, I had an interest with how games were made and this interest became stronger as I got older which led me down the path of learning game development.

Throughout my years of study, I have been able to learn how to break down current game mechanics to understand what makes them fun and design my own mechanics to replace them.

I’ve worked with multiple game engines along with the languages associated with them to create unique gameplay features, engaging experiences, and fun and engaging experiences.

I’ve learned how to work within a team and how as a team leader I should work to manage and delegate tasks correctly whilst simultaneously providing constructive feedback and support to the team.
