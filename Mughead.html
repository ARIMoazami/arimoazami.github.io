Mughead

This video displays the atari 2600 demake of the floral fury boss fight from Cuphead.

Game Description: The boss fight has multiple phases which change based on boss health with different attacks for each phase. The player will have 3 total lives and no extra lives will drop during the entire boss fight. Once the boss is defeated, the player will gain score and the boss will respawn with different changes such as increase health and quicker attacks.

This was designed and created with 5 others in a short 2 week game jam.

Contributions:

  • Project Lead
  • Producer
  • Implemented player movement and shooting
  • Created main menu and pause menu
  • Implemented boss sprite animations and state switching
  • Added changes to boss behaviour after death and respawning
